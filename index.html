Take a look at Photobox.

Photobox is a virtual shoebox for your photos — use it to store pictures on your iPhone or iPad that are kept separate from your photo library. Photobox is perfect for work photos, screenshots, semi-private images, and anything else you don’t want lumped in with the built-in Photos app.

Photos App Friendly

Photobox can import pictures from your Photos app, and export them back to your photo library, too.

Paste Like a Pro

Any pictures you’ve copied to the clipboard from another app can be pasted and saved into Photobox.

In Tune with iTunes

Connect your iPhone or iPad to iTunes, then easily drag and drop photos directly into Photobox.

Save from Any App

You can use any app that supports iOS actions to send photos directly to Photobox. Just tap the standard share button and choose “Save to Photobox.”

Modern and Minimal

Photobox is built for iOS 8 and up, featuring a simple interface that keeps the focus on your photos.
